# Fund Transfer: NEFT vs IMPS

We support two interbank transfer rails for fund transfers: NEFT and IMPS.

**NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer)**
- Settlement: processed in half-hourly batches, Monday to Saturday 8 AM – 7 PM (excluding bank holidays)
- Availability: not available on Sundays and bank holidays
- Limit: no upper limit per transaction (subject to account limits)
- Charges: free for amounts up to Rs 10,000; Rs 2.50 for Rs 10,001–Rs 1 lakh; Rs 5 for Rs 1 lakh–Rs 2 lakh; Rs 15 for above Rs 2 lakh
- Best for: large, non-urgent transfers

**IMPS (Immediate Payment Service)**
- Settlement: real-time, 24×7 including Sundays and bank holidays
- Limit: up to Rs 5 lakh per transaction
- Charges: Rs 5 per transaction up to Rs 1 lakh; Rs 15 for Rs 1 lakh–Rs 2 lakh; Rs 25 for above Rs 2 lakh
- Best for: urgent transfers, after-hours payments

To initiate a transfer: select your payee, enter the amount, choose NEFT or IMPS, review the confirmation, and enter the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.
