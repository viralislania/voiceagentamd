# How to Open a Recurring Deposit

A recurring deposit (RD) lets you invest a fixed installment every month for a chosen tenure, earning interest higher than a savings account.

We offer two RD products: Recurring 12m (6.8% for 12 months, minimum Rs 500/month) and Recurring 24m (7.0% for 24 months, minimum Rs 500/month). Maximum installment is Rs 1,00,000 per month for both.

Installments are auto-debited from your linked savings account on the booking date each month. Missing an installment incurs a penalty of Rs 10 per Rs 1,000 of the installment.

Premature closure of an RD is allowed after 3 months; a 1% penalty on the applicable interest rate applies. Interest is paid at maturity along with the principal.

To open an RD, select the product, set your monthly installment amount, confirm the tenure, and authorize via OTP.
