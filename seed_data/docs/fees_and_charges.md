# Fees and Charges

If the average monthly balance falls below Rs 5000, a penalty of Rs 150 per month is applied to savings accounts.

Debit card issuance is free for the first year; an annual fee of Rs 200 applies thereafter. There is no charge for NEFT or IMPS transfers made through the app.

Cheque book requests beyond the first free booklet per year cost Rs 50 per booklet.
