# Transaction Failure FAQ

Transfers can fail for several reasons. The most common are insufficient funds, exceeding the daily transaction limit, the beneficiary bank being temporarily unavailable, or a network timeout at the switch.

If your account was debited but the transfer failed, the amount is usually auto-reversed within 48 hours. If it is not reversed, you can raise a complaint and the ticket is resolved within 48 hours.

To reduce failures, ensure sufficient balance, verify beneficiary details, and avoid retrying immediately during a bank outage.
