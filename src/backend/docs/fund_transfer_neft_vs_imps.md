# Fund Transfer: NEFT vs IMPS

We support two interbank transfer rails: NEFT and IMPS.

**NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer)**
- Settlement: half-hourly batches, Mon–Sat 8 AM–7 PM (excluding bank holidays).
- Not available on Sundays or public holidays.
- No upper limit per transaction (subject to account-level daily limits).
- Charges: free up to Rs 10,000; Rs 2.50 for Rs 10,001–1 lakh; Rs 5 for 1–2 lakh; Rs 15 above 2 lakh.
- Best for: large or non-urgent transfers.

**IMPS (Immediate Payment Service)**
- Settlement: real-time, 24×7 including Sundays and bank holidays.
- Limit: up to Rs 5 lakh per transaction.
- Charges: Rs 5 up to Rs 1 lakh; Rs 15 for 1–2 lakh; Rs 25 above 2 lakh.
- Best for: urgent transfers or after-hours payments.

To transfer: select a payee, enter the amount, choose NEFT or IMPS, review the confirmation card, and enter the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.
