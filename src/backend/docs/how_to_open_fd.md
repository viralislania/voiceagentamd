# How to Open a Fixed Deposit

A fixed deposit (FD) locks a lump sum for a fixed tenure in exchange for a higher interest rate than a savings account. You can open an FD from your existing savings account in the app.

We offer three products: Short-Term FD (6.5% for 6 months), Standard FD (7.1% for 12 months), and Long-Term FD (7.4% for 36 months). Minimum deposit is Rs 10000 for Short-Term and Standard, and Rs 25000 for Long-Term.

Interest is paid at maturity. Premature withdrawal of an FD is allowed but incurs a 1% penalty on the applicable interest rate.
