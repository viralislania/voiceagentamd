# How to Open a Recurring Deposit

A recurring deposit (RD) lets you invest a fixed amount every month for a chosen tenure, earning interest higher than a savings account.

We offer two RD products: Recurring 12m (6.8% p.a. for 12 months) and Recurring 24m (7.0% p.a. for 24 months). Minimum installment is Rs 500 per month; maximum is Rs 1,00,000 per month.

Installments are auto-debited from your linked savings account on the booking date each month. A missed installment incurs a penalty of Rs 10 per Rs 1,000 of the installment amount.

Premature closure is allowed after 3 months; a 1% penalty on the applicable interest rate is charged. Interest is paid at maturity together with the principal.

To open an RD: select the product, set your monthly installment amount, confirm the tenure, and authorise via OTP.
