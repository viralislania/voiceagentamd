# KYC Requirements

KYC (Know Your Customer) is mandatory to open any account. A PAN card is required for all accounts. For identity and address, an Aadhaar card is accepted as a single document covering both.

If Aadhaar is not used, you must provide one proof of identity (passport or voter ID) and one separate proof of address (utility bill or rental agreement no older than three months).

Video KYC can be completed in the app and usually takes under ten minutes.
