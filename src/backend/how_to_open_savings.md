# How to Open a Savings Account

To open a savings account you need: a PAN card, one proof of identity (Aadhaar, passport, or voter ID), and one proof of address. Minimum opening deposit is Rs 1000.

The account is usually activated within one working day after document verification. You can apply directly in the app, and an agent will call to complete video KYC if required.

A savings account requires an average monthly balance of Rs 5000. Falling below this attracts a penalty (see fees and charges).
